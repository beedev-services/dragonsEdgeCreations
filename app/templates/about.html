CAMILLE EDGE

Camille Edge grew up in South Carolina and lived in Boston for many years later moving back. She is not only a writer and artist, but also a computer geek with many years in the IT, training, and technical writing fields during the day while at night emotions and feelings transform into art.

She is a cross-functional collaborator with recognized abilities in technical communication, curriculum development, training, facilitating, leadership, and user testing. She thrives as a subject matter expert and grasps all angles of challenging situations.

Camille helps technical communication managers and learning and development leaders at organizations virtually achieve growth with their employees by creating efficient and easy to understand documents and successfully training employees by creating informative and engaging content.

She volunteered her time as a Technical Consultant & Web Developer for two non-profits as well as a project manager for a Wonsulting project. Camille has launched Dragons Edge Creations LLC.

MY ART

Camille Edge has enjoyed creating abstract art and taking photographs for years. Her pieces express the feelings and emotions of everyday life. Her art can be viewed from any direction, angle, or distance. What you see up close may change as you move away. There is no top or bottom. Turn the images and see what comes to mind. Everyone has a different perspective. Let your imagination run free.

She uses a pencil, pen, marker, and watercolors in her art. Camille enjoys landscape photography by finding different angles and perspectives.

Camille is a poet and writer. She is preparing a children’s book for publication and writing a fantasy book. She co-authored a nonfiction Climate Report while in college.
